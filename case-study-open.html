CASE STUDY

Rapid VPN Circumvention Control

How a national regulator reduced VPN circumvention in minutes instead of days.

Industry
Government & Regulatory
Region
National (Multi-Region)
Solution
DPI Circumvention Control

Challenge

VPN and proxy technologies rapidly increase, making enforcement difficult. Traditional methods cause over-blocking and service disruption.

Solution

DPI enables region-based detection, traffic classification, and centralized policy deployment with operator verification.

Outcome

  • Successful circumvention reduced
  • Transparent execution reporting
  • Policy deployment completed within minutes

The Story

A national regulator faced a surge in VPN and proxy usage that made enforcement slow and ineffective. DPI was deployed to restore control with precision and speed.

Using AI-powered detection and regional policy enforcement, the regulator was able to apply targeted rules across all operators without impacting critical services. Real-time dashboards ensured full visibility and fast response.

The result: effective circumvention control, minimal service disruption, and faster decision-making backed by data.

Reduction in response time (from days to hours)

99.9%
Availability of critical services maintained
